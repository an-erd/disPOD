	START
	---
	Register Callbacks
	Register App Profiles
	---
	Set scan parameter
	---
	Start scanning
	until Device = MilestonePod or timeout
	---
	Connect to Device
	Connect ok?			-> handle, retry, ...
	---
	Configure MTU Size
	Search Services
	Get Characteristics
	Register for Notification
	---
	END
